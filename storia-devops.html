📖 Storia: The DevOps Journey

Each stage is a chapter in the continuous story of development and delivery.

🧠
Chapter 1: Plan
The idea is born. I outline the goals, understand user needs, and sketch the story I want to tell.
🖋️
Chapter 2: Develop
I begin crafting the code — writing each line like a sentence in my unfolding story.
🔧
Chapter 3: Build
All pieces come together, transforming ideas into a working product.
🧪
Chapter 4: Test
The draft is reviewed. Bugs are caught before it hits the readers (users).
🚀
Chapter 5: Release
The polished story is ready to be published and shared with the world.
📦
Chapter 6: Deploy
The app enters the world stage, available to all — fast and automated.
⚙️
Chapter 7: Operate
Operations ensure the story runs smoothly with no interruptions.
📊
Chapter 8: Monitor
Feedback flows in. We listen, observe, and prepare for the next chapter.
